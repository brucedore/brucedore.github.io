I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Communication Neuroscience lab at the University of Pennsylvania. My research focuses on the social and affective mechanisms that shape individual- and population-level behaviors. One line of my work examines psychological and neural mechanisms of persuasion and idea propagation. A second focuses on the social regulation of emotion and the processes of social influence that enable this regulation. A third pays specific attention to neural mechanisms of emotional reactivity and regulation.
